Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson is the clumsy father of the Simpson family. With his wife, Marge, he has three children: Bart, a son he doesn't like, and Lisa and Maggie, two girls. To support his family, he works at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. Homer embodies several comical stereotypes of working-class Americans: he is rude, overweight, incompetent, rude, lazy, ignorant and loves to eat donuts; however, he is very dedicated to his family.

Bart Simpson

Bartholomew Jo-Jo Jay Simpson Bartholomew J. Simpson, also known as "Bart Simpson", or just "Bart", is the eldest son of the Simpson family. The character is portrayed as a rebellious and disobedient boy who has bad grades at school. This behavior often leaves him in difficult situations with people who work at his school, with his family, and with strangers. Additionally, he loves skateboarding.

Lisa Simpson

Lisa Marie Simpson is one of the protagonists of the series The Simpsons. He lives at 742 Evergreen Terrace with his father, Homer, his mother, Marge, and his brothers, Bart and Maggie. She is an 8-year-old girl who has an extremely high intelligence and likes to play the saxophone and her biggest idol is Murphy Bloody Gums.

Marge Simpson

Marge is the wife of Homer Simpson and mother of Lisa, Bart and Maggie Simpson in the animated series The Simpsons. She is best known for her long blue hair, whose hairstyle was inspired by the film The Bride of Frankenstein and her very patient personality. Even though Homer gets into countless troubles, she continues to be a faithful and dedicated wife, just as she is towards her children.

Maggie Simpson

Maggie is a character that appears in the cartoon The Simpsons. She is the youngest daughter of the main characters Homer Simpson and Marge, and forever a baby. Her fame is due to her often falling to the ground trying to walk and sucking on her pacifier. Maggie is named after creator Matt Groening's younger sister, Margaret "Maggie" Groening.
